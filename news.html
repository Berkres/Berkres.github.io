September 2024

New concept of releasing music

Berkres decided to change things up a bit. First of all, the cover art looks different now, no release title, just his name. But why is there another image with a black frame? The one with the white frame will be just pure piano (in most cases). After Berkres was experimenting with different effects, he couldn't decide whether he should release the pure piano or the more experimental one and because Berkres - to this day - still couldn't decide, he came up with the idea of simply uploading both versions. Berkres calls the experimental version Special Edition.
If you have trouble understanding this new concept, then you can scroll further down.

Upcoming Release - 02:03

02:03 - that was the night when Berkres experimented with different effects. With this name, Berkres wants to highlight the beginning of something new. This will be the soft and pure piano version.

Upcoming Release - 02:03 (Special Edition)

Since this release is experimental, Berkres calls it 02:03 (Special Edition) as explained in the very first article. What exactly does experimental mean? Well, you'll have to find out when the Special Edition is out...
