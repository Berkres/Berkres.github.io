All About Me and My Love for Piano

When people ask me what instrument I play, I proudly reply with "Piano". But who am I?
I am Berkres, a person whose life is all about playing his instrument without ever getting bored. I could never get bored. These night sessions, sitting in front of the piano looking out the window and playing whatever my soul wants to.

My piano journey started when I was 7 years old, a little boy who started to learn how to write. Over the years, the piano became a crucial part of my life. One day, I found a really nice sounding chord, it was a D♭maj7 chord to be more specific. That inspired me to make my own piece - "Hope?". And from that day, I started creating my own music.

Music isn't just about writing a melody. Music is a language. A language that everyone understands.
A language that everyone can speak. A language that can speak words no other language can. Music always tells a story. A story than can also be represented with a specific color and mood. How many colors are there? Many, more than 10 million. Each of those 10 million colors belongs to a piece of music and a type of mood. That's why abstract art inspires me so much, because you can interpret everything. I want my listeners to make their own music listening experience, and not being dependant on the artwork or the music itself. The music just helps to draw your imagination in your mind and that's why I prefer to write music that is open and provides room for inspiration.
