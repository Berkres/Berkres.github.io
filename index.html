Welcome to Berkres' Official Website!

I'm glad you found my official artist website. Here you'll find everything about my work as an artist, from current projects to sneak peeks. I really enjoy creating music that combines both violin and piano. My work is known for my soft and unique piano sound and my melodic violins. I draw inspiration from abstract paintings that hide the meaning behind it.

If you'd like to learn more about me and my approach to music, then take a look at the "About" page.
In the "Gallery" page, you can see all of my works in one place. Take your time to explore my music.
Talking about music, the "News" page is where you can stay informed about my latest releases, upcoming videos and sheet music uploads.
Lastly, if you have any questions or just want to say hello, don't hesitate to visit the "Contact" page. There, you can reach out to me directly for any inquiries or collaborations.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy exploring my website!
