Welcome to Berkres' Official Website!

I'm glad you've visited my official artist website. Here you'll find everything about my work as an artist, from current projects to my artistic journey. I am an artist specializing in creating music that combines both violin and piano. My work is known for my soft piano sound and my melodic violins. I draw inspiration from abstract paintings that hide the meaning and message behind it.
If you'd like to learn more about me and my approach to music, then take a look at the "About" page.
